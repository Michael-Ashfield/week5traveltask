Iceland

Iceland is a country of extreme contrasts. Widely known as “The Land of Fire and Ice”. Iceland is home to some of the largest glaciers in Europe, and some of the world's most active volcanoes. Iceland is also the land of light and darkness. Long summer days with nearly 24-hours of sunshine are offset by short winter days with only few hours of daylight.
